Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for global buyers

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

For our stock items, we support an MOQ of just 1 piece. For custom logo orders, the MOQ typically starts at 100 sets.

Can I get a free sample before placing a bulk order?

Yes! We provide free samples for verified business buyers. You only need to cover the express shipping cost.

Do you support custom logo laser engraving?

Absolutely. We have high-precision laser marking machines in-house to engrave your brand logo on any cutlery item.

What is your typical production lead time?

For stock items, we ship within 3-5 days. For custom OEM orders, production usually takes 15-25 days depending on the volume.

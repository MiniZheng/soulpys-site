A Legacy of Quality

Jieyang SOULPYS Stainless Steel Cutlery Factory is a professional cutlery flatware factory. Our core teammates have been in this industry for over 20 years, so we are very experienced.

We know how to make a product exactly the way you want it to be. Our main products are stainless steel cutlery, flatware, chopsticks, measuring scoops and kitchen utensils.

We take pride in our strict quality control and thoughtful customer service. Our products are selling well in the USA, UK, Germany, Malaysia, Brazil, and Thailand.
